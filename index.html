PROCESS

From Idea
to Instrument.

Phase 01

Research & Discovery

Conducted a 100+ person user survey. Mapped limitations of traditional rulers, protractors, and compasses. Analyzed patents US10955232B2 and WO2022059036A2 to identify existing gaps.

Survey · Patent Analysis · Literature Review
Phase 02

Architecture & Design

Defined the modular layered architecture. Created initial 3D CAD models in SolidWorks.

SolidWorks · CAD
Phase 03

Electronics Integration

Integrated the OLED display module, Bluetooth communication layer, and Li-ion battery.

OLED · BLE · Li-ion
Phase 04

Prototyping

3D-printed the initial housing with the compass locking teeth mechanism. Assembled slider track, LED array for partition marking, and roller-wheel parallel-line support.

3D Printing · FDM · Assembly
Phase 05

Final Fabrication

Sensor calibration, mode-switching logic, partition computation, and Bluetooth data export pipeline — all validated on the physical prototype.

C · Integration · Validation
